Worship

In Truth and Spirit

We encourage you to learn the act of pure undiluted worship. The art of giving thanks to God through music and poring out your soul in honor of His glory.

Prayers

Be Intentional

Prayers are our means of communing with God in heaven and it is something that must be done with full reverence and sobreity of mind.

Love

Selflessness is It

Show love and be kind to everyone, even when you feel they do not deserve it. It is God's commandment we must obey to live a happy and fulfilled life.

A Christian should live for the glory of God and the well-being of others.

As a Christain you must always think above the level of self, and understand that you have been called to a life of service in the Lord's vineyard.

Welcome to Salvation Church

Connect, Grow and Serve with Us

Wapogom Evangelical Ministries, is a movement that aims to break the yoke of sin that is causing a barrier between people and true communion with God.

We do not belong to any Church or body, rather we believe in seeing the body of Christ as a whole, preaching the Gospel to whoever is ready to listen.
